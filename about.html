WHO WE ARE

Boost Your Property Management Efficiency with REMS

REMS is a comprehensive online property management software that enables real estate agents and landlords to efficiently manage their properties, including property information, maintenance requests, and invoicing. Features

Property Management

Easily track and manage all your properties, including property details, lease agreements, and payment history.

Maintenance Requests

Allow tenants to submit maintenance requests directly through the platform. Get notifications and manage repairs in one place.

Financial Tools

Automatically generate invoices, track rent payments, and manage your property's finances effortlessly.

Tenant Communication

Stay connected with tenants through in-app messaging, notifications, and reminders about important events like rent payments and maintenance schedules.

How It Works

Our platform is user-friendly and designed for ease of use. Here’s how it works:

  1. Sign Up: Create an account and set up your properties.
  2. Manage Properties: Upload property details, set rent prices, and track payment history.
  3. Receive Requests: Tenants can submit maintenance or other service requests directly through the platform.
  4. Invoicing & Payments: Automatically generate invoices for your tenants and track payments.
  5. Reports: Get detailed reports on your properties’ performance and financial status.
